Q: What is PHIERS?
PHIERS (The People's Health Insurance Endowment & Referral Service) solves three things Congress says America needs with ONE system: affordable healthcare for individuals, AI-resistant jobs, and UBI monthly assistance. How? By allowing CMS to cover telehealth as a legitimate insurance alternative, which saves the federal government TRILLIONS annually. We contract with government to deliver those savings.
Q: How much does telehealth cost vs traditional insurance?
Traditional insurance: $8,500+/year individuals, $24,000+/year for higher-cost plans. PHIERS telehealth: $600/year. Individual saves $7,400-$23,400 annually. 80% of healthcare visits don't require hospitalization or specialty care—telehealth covers everything you actually need.
