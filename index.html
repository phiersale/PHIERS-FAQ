The Basics

Q: What is PHIERS?

A: The People's Health Insurance Endowment and Referral Service - a cooperative economic model that solves America's affordability crisis by replacing expensive traditional insurance ($8,500+/year) with affordable telehealth ($600/year) for the 80% who don't need hospitalization coverage.

The Affordability Solution: $7,900 annual savings per person. At 3.5% participation, FREE health insurance activates for all members.

Q: How does PHIERS solve the affordability crisis?

  • Telehealth Substitution: 80% of Americans don't need expensive hospital coverage. PHIERS provides $600/year telehealth ($50/month) vs $8,500+ traditional insurance = $7,900 savings per person.

Combined: $2.73 trillion in annual national savings funds universal coverage, 5-10 million jobs, and creates mathematical leverage politicians cannot ignore.

How It Works

Perfect for: The 80% who only visit doctors occasionally and don't need hospital coverage. If you're generally healthy, PHIERS saves you $7,900+ annually.
