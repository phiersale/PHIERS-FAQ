PHIERS FAQ

Common Questions About Healthcare, Jobs & UBI

Q: What is PHIERS?
PHIERS solves three things Congress wants with ONE system: affordable healthcare for individuals, AI-resistant jobs, and UBI. How? By allowing CMS to cover telehealth ($600/year vs $8,500+ insurance), saving TRILLIONS annually. We contract to deliver those savings.
Q: How much does telehealth cost vs traditional insurance?
Traditional: $8,500+/year individuals. PHIERS telehealth: $600/year. Individual saves $7,400-$23,400 annually. 80% of healthcare visits don't need hospitalization—telehealth covers everything you actually need.
Q: Where does TRILLIONS come from?
Federal healthcare spending: $1.8+ trillion annually. Private insurance: $1.6+ trillion. If 234M individuals switch to $600/year telehealth, government saves $1+ TRILLION. Private markets save $800B+. Total: $2+ TRILLION in annual savings.
Q: When can this start?
Congress is home Dec 16-31. If we organize at scale and Congress allows CMS telehealth adoption, benefits can start immediately. First members could begin healthcare within weeks. Housing and UBI follow within 2-3 months.
Q: Is this proven to work?
Yes. Mark Cuban's Cost Plus: 7M individuals, 80-90% savings. ZORTT Healthcare: Operational 1+ year, proven delivery. Congress voted 231-195 when organized. Chenoweth: 3.5% organized = guaranteed change. We target 100M+ (9x threshold).
